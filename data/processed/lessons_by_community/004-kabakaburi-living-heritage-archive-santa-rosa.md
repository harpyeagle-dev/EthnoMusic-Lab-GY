# Kabakaburi Living Heritage Archive (Santa Rosa)

> This module integrates hinterland community knowledge, land-based listening, and local instrument craft traditions.

- **Tradition:** Community song and dance practice
- **Community:** Santa Rosa
- **Region:** Upper Pomeroon
- **Language:** Guyanese English

## Target Communities
- Kabakaburi
- Kamarang/Warawatta
- Waramaong
- Santa Rosa

## Learning Objectives
- Document traditional songs, recordings, performances, and sonic heritage

## Activities
- Explore traditional songs, recordings, performances, and sonic heritage

## Localized Examples
- Village celebration song cycles in Kamarang/Warawatta and Waramaong
- Handmade percussion from locally available materials in Kabakaburi and Santa Rosa

## Assessment
Learners synthesize source evidence into a culturally grounded performance or analysis task.
