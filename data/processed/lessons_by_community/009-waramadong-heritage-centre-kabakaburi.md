# Waramadong Heritage Centre (Kabakaburi)

> This module integrates hinterland community knowledge, land-based listening, and local instrument craft traditions.

- **Tradition:** Intergenerational rhythm transmission
- **Community:** Kabakaburi
- **Region:** Upper Mazaruni
- **Language:** Guyanese English

## Instruments
- voice

## Target Communities
- Kabakaburi
- Kamarang/Warawatta
- Waramaong
- Santa Rosa

## Learning Objectives
- Identify key musical traits documented in this community source
- Connect musical practice to local cultural context

## Activities
- Explore stories, songs, traditions and community knowledge
- Listen to elders, youth and community perspectives

## Localized Examples
- Village celebration song cycles in Kamarang/Warawatta and Waramaong
- Handmade percussion from locally available materials in Kabakaburi and Santa Rosa

## Assessment
Learners synthesize source evidence into a culturally grounded performance or analysis task.
