# Consent Checklist

- Confirm the participant understands the purpose of the study.
- Confirm whether recording is allowed for research use.
- Confirm whether excerpts may be shared in class, publication, or archive contexts.
- Record any restrictions on names, faces, places, or ceremonial content.
- Note whether the participant prefers anonymization or attribution.
- Store any special conditions in the `ethics` section of `docs/annotation_schema.yaml`.
