## Final Notes

This digital curriculum represents a complete, ready-to-implement educational framework for teaching digital humanities approaches to cultural heritage. It balances technical skill development with cultural understanding and ethical practice.

The curriculum honors the Baboon Dance tradition while demonstrating how computational methods can complement (not replace) traditional ethnomusicological approaches. By centering ethics, community benefit, and critical reflection, it models responsible digital humanities pedagogy.

**The materials are ready to use, adaptable to your needs, and designed to support transformative learning at the intersection of culture, technology, and ethics.**
